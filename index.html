FAQ

Get answers to commonly asked questions about Ready 3D.

How to download a model?

To download a 3D model, open the models page and look for the Download button below the viewer. If the creator has allowed downloads, youll see available file formats (such as .obj, .fbx, or .glb). Select your preferred format and the download will start automatically. If theres no download option, it means the author has restricted access to the model in that case, you can either contact the creator directly or look for similar models with open download permissions.
